Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, canberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

INGREDIENTS:

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
